Concept Kernel Protocol

How a Material Entity wakes into being, executes its purpose, and accumulates knowledge.

A Concept Kernel is a BFO:0000040 Material Entity with three independently-versioned loops: identity (CK), capability (TOOL), and knowledge (DATA).

Three Loops

  • CK Loop — Who am I and why am I? Identity & Awakening
  • TOOL Loop — What can I do? Executable Capability
  • DATA Loop — What have I produced? Knowledge & Production
